ABOUT ME

UI/UX DESIGNER || PRODUCT DESIGNER

I am an engineering student specializing in Food Processing Engineering (Applied Chemical Engineering) in the University of Ghana, Legon. I've been a video editor for about 2years now and during these years, I was introduced to UI/UX design by a friend so I decided to read more about it. I loved it immediately because of how it deals with creativity! and brainstorm!. I am very passionate about my work and I love doing it. I am a team player and a very reliable person. I love to learn from people and also to teach people what I know. Data tells us "what", but users tell us "why". I am passionate about building experiences that solves the "why" for users, and demonstrate the engineering behind how it works.
